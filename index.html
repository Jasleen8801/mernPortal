Ontario Health Insurance Plan (OHIP) Coverage Document List

Required Documents for OHIP Coverage

  • Canadian Citizenship or Immigration Status:
    • Canadian birth certificate, Canadian passport, or Canadian citizenship card
    • Permanent Resident Card (PR Card) or Record of Landing
    • Work permit, study permit, or other immigration documents
  • Proof of Ontario Residency:
    • Ontario driver's license or Ontario Photo Card
    • Utility bill or lease agreement in your name
    • Ontario Health Card from another province (for transferring coverage)
  • Proof of Identity:
    • Passport or driver's license
    • Birth certificate or citizenship card
  • Valid Immigration Documents:
    • Valid work permit or study permit
    • Refugee Protection Claimant Document
    • Temporary Resident Permit
  • Supporting Documents:
    • Marriage certificate or divorce papers (if applicable)
    • Legal name change certificate (if applicable)
